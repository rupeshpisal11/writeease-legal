Privacy Policy for WriteEase AI

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WriteEase AI ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app. WriteEase AI is developed and maintained by an independent developer based in India. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

TypeWhat it isWhy we collect it
Text you enter into a writing tool The text you type or paste into the Paraphraser, AI Humanizer, Grammar Fixer, Email Writer, Templates, or Plagiarism Checker Sent to our AI processing partner (Groq) solely to generate the tool's output. Not stored on any server we control.
Usage & history data Your daily credit count, tool usage history, dark mode preference, premium status Stored locally on your device only, to run features like your daily free-credit limit and your writing history. This data never leaves your device and is not visible to us.
Advertising identifier Android Advertising ID and device/ad interaction data Collected by Google AdMob to show and measure ads. We do not control or access this data directly — see Section 3.
Purchase information Confirmation that you purchased the Premium upgrade (₹99 one-time) Handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We only receive a purchase-confirmed status, not your payment details.

WriteEase AI does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or phone number directly within the app.

2. How Your Text Is Processed

When you use a writing tool, the text you enter is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to Groq (groq.com), a third-party AI inference provider, solely to generate the requested output (e.g., a paraphrased version, a grammar correction). We do not permanently store this text on any server we operate. Please avoid entering sensitive personal information (such as financial details, passwords, or medical information) into the writing tools, since it is transmitted to a third-party service for processing.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Your writing history, usage counters, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's app storage. This data is deleted automatically if you uninstall the app or manually clear the app's storage/cache. We do not maintain our own backend database of user content.

5. Children's Privacy

WriteEase AI is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can address it.

6. Your Choices

  • You can clear your local history and usage data at any time by clearing the app's storage in Android Settings, or by uninstalling the app.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's Google Ads Settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • You can request account/data deletion assistance by contacting us — see Section 8.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all network communication between the app and our AI processing partner. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
